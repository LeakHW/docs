# Codebase AI Compatiability
Our codebase has multiple features to make it compatiable with AI

---

## Knowledgebase
Our codebase has a knowledgebase that AIs can read and write to to store and retrieve knowledge;.

---

## Logbook
Our codebase has a logbook that AIs can read and write to to keep track of what AIs make what changes and when. This is for maximum transparency.
